HOW TO PLAY

🎮 CONTROLS

📱 Mobile — Tap the 4 arrow buttons at bottom

⌨️ PC — Arrow Keys or WASD

⏸ Pause — ESC or ⏸ button

🎵 GAMEPLAY

  • ▸ Notes travel toward the receptors
  • ▸ Press matching arrow at the right moment
  • ▸ Hit → BF HP up  ·  Miss → BF HP down
  • ▸ BF bar hits zero → GAME OVER!
  • ▸ Survive the whole song → WIN!

    • ❤️ HEALTH BAR

  • ▸ 🔵 Left (blue) = Opponent HP
  • ▸ 🔴 Right (pink) = BF HP
  • ▸ Center dot slides toward the winner
  • ▸ Winning character emoji glows!

    • ⭐ RATINGS

  • 🔵 SICK! — Perfect (+100 pts)
  • 🟢 GOOD — Good timing (+75 pts)
  • 🟡 BAD — Slightly off (+25 pts)
  • 🔴 MISS — Missed or ghost tap (−HP)
  • 🟣 HOLD — Press & hold for +15 pts/tick!

    • ⚡ COMBO MULTIPLIER

  • ▸ 0–9 combo → ×1.0 (normal)
  • ▸ 10–19 combo → ×1.5 (BOOSTED!)
  • ▸ 20+ combo → ×2.0 (MAX!)
  • ▸ Missing a note resets multiplier to ×1.0

    • 📊 TIME BAR v0.1.6

  • ▸ Centered timer shows elapsed time
  • ▸ Right side shows remaining time
  • ▸ Bar color: 🟢 → 🟡 → 🔴 as song ends
